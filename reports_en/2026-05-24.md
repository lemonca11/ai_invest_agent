## Confirmed Events

### Event 1: Anthropic publishes first-month Project Glasswing results

- **Company / Product**: Anthropic / Claude Mythos Preview
- **AI layer**: models
- **Event type**: model capability update / cybersecurity breakthrough
- **Published at**: 2026-05-22
- **Summary**: Anthropic said Mythos Preview helped around 50 partners find more than 10,000 high-severity or critical vulnerabilities during the first month of Project Glasswing. Cloudflare reportedly found 2,000 vulnerabilities, Mozilla found 271 vulnerabilities in Firefox 150 testing, and scans across more than 1,000 open-source projects found an estimated 6,202 high-severity or critical vulnerabilities with a 90.6% true-positive rate after independent security-company validation.
- **Direct impact**: AI-driven vulnerability discovery appears to be moving from lab demonstrations toward commercial deployment. The new bottleneck may shift from finding vulnerabilities to remediating them.
- **Investment implication**: Anthropic is building a strong position in enterprise AI security, with potential pressure on OpenAI, Google, and cybersecurity platforms. The Claude Compliance API ecosystem also continues to expand through security integrations.
- **Caveat**: The event is based mainly on Anthropic's official disclosure. It should not be treated as an industry-wide conclusion until third-party validation and competitor responses are available.
- **Confidence**: High on official disclosure, with single-source limitations.
- **Source URL**: https://www.anthropic.com/research/glasswing-initial-update
